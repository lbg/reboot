Get hands-on at our Workshops 

We will be running workshops throughout the day, providing you with the opportunity to get hands-on with technology. If you are looking to attend a workshop, visit the room at the start time listed below and get involved! No advance sign-up is required. Our sponsors Microsoft will also be hosting Express Insights sessions throughout the day.

Please note: some workshops require you to bring your LBG Laptop. Please take care with bank devices at this event and always keep them with you.

Discover our Exhibitors 

In the Exchange Hall today, you will find multiple exhibition stalls from suppliers, partners, and areas of the Group. Take the opportunity to speak to one of our experts and get hands-on with an activity or demo.

The Reboot Awards 🏅

Our Reboot Awards are returning for a 2nd year, celebrating inspiring colleagues across learning and delivery! We are delighted to have Ron, our CIOs and our WCT community sponsoring our awards who will be picking the winners from our shortlisted nominations!

Join us in Exchange 9 – Upper Level at 15:45 as we announce our winners.

Career Coaching Chats

At Reboot Manchester, five career coaches will be available, holding 1:1 sessions throughout the day. Sessions will be hosted in the area with Pillars between the Charter Foyer and The Gallery. Colleagues attending will have pre-registered for the sessions, but slots might be available so feel free to visit to see if any slots become available. (Please refer to floor plan for signposting)
